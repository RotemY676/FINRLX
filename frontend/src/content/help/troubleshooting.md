Troubleshooting is organized symptom-first: pick the thing you are seeing, and the page walks through likely causes ranked by frequency and what to check next. The most common symptoms include "my recommendation disappeared," "the backtest is unrealistically good," "data freshness is unavailable," and "a breach won't clear."

The full troubleshooting catalogue is populated in a later phase.
