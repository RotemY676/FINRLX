Every recommendation in FINRLX carries the full chain of evidence used to produce it: the data snapshot, the feature transforms, the engine version, the policy controls in force, and the audit trail of overlay decisions. The Replay screen lets you reconstruct any past recommendation exactly as it was at decision time.

This page explains what provenance means in the product, how to read the audit trail, and the guarantees the system makes about reproducibility.
