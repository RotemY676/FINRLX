The News page surfaces external news links (Yahoo Finance, MarketWatch, and others) filtered to names in your universe. The page is intentionally minimal — the workspace does not analyze sentiment or generate trade signals from news.

## What this page is

- A convenience pane for keeping an eye on coverage of names you hold or are considering.
- A starting point for manual research that complements the model's view.

## What this page is not

- A signal generator. News is *not* a feature in the engine's state vector.
- An exhaustive feed. The page surfaces a curated subset; serious news monitoring belongs in a dedicated tool.
