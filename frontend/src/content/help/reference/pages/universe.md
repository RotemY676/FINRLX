The Universe page is where you manage lists of tradable assets. Universes are point-in-time — see [Universe and features](/help/concepts/universe-and-features).

## Sections

### Universes list

Every universe available to you, with name, member count, last membership update, and survivorship-verification status. Click a row to load detail.

### US Large Cap (detail)

When a universe is loaded, this panel shows: the active membership list as of today, with sector, last price, average daily volume, and inclusion-date.

### Coverage

Per-asset chip indicating whether the data feed is complete. UNAVAILABLE on a row means a feed has been silent for too long; the asset will not be included in this cycle's recommendations.

### Readiness

Per-asset chip indicating whether enough history is available for the feature stack. A newly-added asset starts at "Warming up" and transitions to "Ready" after the lookback window has been populated.

### Sector breakdown

Bar chart of asset counts (or aggregated market cap) by sector. Useful when comparing two universes for sector exposure.

## Actions

- **Add asset** — appends a name with effective-date.
- **Remove asset** — removes a name with effective-date.
- **Verify survivorship** — runs the membership-history check against the source of record.
