The Research hub at [`/research`](/research) is the entry point for company-level analysis. It lists the tickers in your active universe and links each one to a dedicated workspace.

## What this page is

- A directory of tickers you have research on (or could open research for).
- The shortest path into a per-ticker workspace — click any ticker to open [`/research/[ticker]`](/help/reference/pages/research-ticker), the per-name page that combines price chart, news, fundamentals, peer comparison, uploaded documents, and AI-generated quarterly insights.

## What this page is not

- A trading screen. There are no order buttons here. The Research hub is about understanding a name, not transacting on it.
- A universe editor. To change which names appear here, go to [Universe](/help/reference/pages/universe).

## How to use it

1. Find the ticker you want to research in the list. Use the search box at the top to filter.
2. Click the ticker to open its workspace. The workspace handles its own data loading — the price chart, news, and (for US-listed names) auto-fetched SEC filings + cross-quarter insights all render progressively as their respective backends respond.
3. To compare two names, open them in separate tabs — the workspace is designed to be glance-able side-by-side.
