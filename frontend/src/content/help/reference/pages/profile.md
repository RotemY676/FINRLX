The Profile page shows the answers you gave in the [welcome wizard](/help/getting-started/understanding-your-profile), the defaults FINRLX derived from them, and the entry point to re-open the wizard.

## Sections

### Identity

Your account information: email, sign-in provider, account creation date.

### Wizard answers

Read-only summary of the answers you submitted in the wizard, grouped by dimension (knowledge, financial, risk, objectives, universe, operational).

### Derived defaults

The defaults FINRLX computed from your answers:

- **Risk profile** — conservative, balanced, or aggressive.
- **Default universe** — attached to new decisions.
- **Default horizon** — the default rebalance horizon.

### Re-run the wizard

A prominent button that reopens the [welcome wizard](/help/getting-started/understanding-your-profile) from the start. Your existing answers are pre-filled; submit again to overwrite.
