The **Research lab** (admin-only) exposes a dataset export that bundles features, raw prices, and the policy controls in force, so you can analyze them in a local notebook. JSONL is the recommended format for large datasets; CSV is fine for ad-hoc spreadsheets.

This guide walks through the export form, the formats available, and what each toggle adds to the bundle.
