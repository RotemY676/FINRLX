Your profile drives the defaults for new decisions: the risk capacity, the default universe, the operational constraints. You can re-run the welcome wizard at any time from the **Profile** page — the "Re-run the wizard" button is the dedicated entry point.

Re-running the wizard is non-destructive; it overwrites your stored answers but does not touch your decision history or paper portfolio.
