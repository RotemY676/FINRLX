Your profile drives the defaults for new decisions: the risk capacity, the default universe, the operational constraints. You can re-run the welcome wizard at any time.

## Steps

1. **Open Profile.** Sidebar → WORKSPACES → My profile (you must be signed in).
2. **Scroll to "Re-run the wizard"** — it is the prominent button below your derived defaults.
3. **Click the button.** The wizard reopens from the start with your existing answers pre-filled.
4. **Step through the questions.** You can change any answer; unchanged answers carry forward.
5. **Submit at the review step.** The server re-derives your risk profile, default universe, and default horizon from the updated answers.

## When you should re-run

- **Your risk capacity has changed.** You took on (or paid off) leverage; a windfall changed your portfolio scale; your time horizon shifted.
- **You want a different default universe.** You started with US large cap and want to include international names; or vice versa.
- **The wizard itself was updated.** When a new wizard version ships, your prior answers may not cover every new dimension. Re-running fills in the new questions.

## What doesn't change

Re-running the wizard is non-destructive:

- **Decision history** stays intact. Past recommendations, replays, and audit entries are untouched.
- **Paper portfolio** keeps its current weights. The wizard updates defaults for *new* decisions; existing positions are not rebalanced.
- **Custom universes** you have built remain. The wizard's "default universe" only changes the universe selected by default for new decisions.
