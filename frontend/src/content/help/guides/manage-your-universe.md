Your universe is the list of tradable assets every engine sees. The **Universe** page shows coverage (do we have data for every name?) and readiness (do we have enough history for the indicator stack?). A common mistake is to add a name and immediately run a backtest before its data is fully populated — readiness will warn you.

This guide explains how to add and remove assets safely, and how to read the coverage and readiness panels.
