---
title: Defer or save a thesis
summary: Park a recommendation for later, or save it as your current working thesis.
diataxis: how-to
area: guides
updated: 2026-05-22
order: 4
---

Not every recommendation is one you want to act on immediately. **Defer** parks the recommendation in the queue so it does not auto-promote on the next cycle; **Save as current thesis** marks it as your working baseline so the comparison views show drift from it.

Both actions are tracked in the audit trail with the time, the user, and the reason if you provide one.
