A breach is raised when a hard constraint cannot be satisfied or when a policy floor is crossed. The **Policies** page lists active breaches; the **Replay** page lets you see the recommendation as it was at the moment the breach was raised.

This guide walks through the standard triage: confirm the breach is real, identify the contributing positions, decide whether to relax the policy or re-derive the recommendation, and clear the breach with an audit-trail note.
