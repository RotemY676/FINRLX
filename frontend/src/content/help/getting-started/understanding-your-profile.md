The welcome wizard collects your knowledge level, risk capacity, objectives, universe preferences, and operational constraints. FINRLX uses these answers to derive your default risk profile, the default universe attached to new decisions, and the policy controls that gate recommendations.

You can re-run the wizard at any time from the **Profile** page — nothing is locked in.
