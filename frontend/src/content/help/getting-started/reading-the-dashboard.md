The Decision Command Center is the home page of the workspace. It surfaces seven panels: Decision queue, Paper portfolio, Opportunity radar, Research events, Research assistant, Governance, and Sector tilt. Each panel has its own provenance chip showing where the data came from and when it was last refreshed.

This page is read-only — every panel deep-links into the relevant detail screen if you want to drill down.
